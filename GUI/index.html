Predictive Analysis for Road Accident Forecasting





AcciTracker is a software solution that can predict accident severity and number of accidents using past accident data.
This system targets people who travel on the road and generates information about accidents
that can happen on that day and the severity of the accident.
Using this information drivers can drive safely and this may be a reason for a reduction in road accidents in the future.
